2020.05.15

沛克咖啡店一歲了！

去年的今天 5/15
是我們第一天的試營運。

還記得那一天的我們，凌晨4、5點還在店內做準備，深怕第一次的開店會準備不周。

那一種緊張感、興奮和期待，在5/15的一天裡，感受特別強烈且印象深刻。
一年過去了，每一天開店遇到的人事物都給了沛克滿滿的能量和啟發，繼續把這個空間變得更好。

Last year today 5/15 It is our first day of trial operation. We still remember that day, we were still preparing in the store at 4 or 5 a.m. I was afraid that the first time we opened the store, we would not prepare well. That kind of tension, excitement and anticipation, in a day of 5/15, felt particularly strong and impressive. A year has passed, and the people and things I met every day to open the store gave Peker full energy and inspiration to continue to make this space better.
